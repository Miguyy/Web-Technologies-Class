Under armour returns policy

If you're not 100% satisfied, you can return your gear for free within 60 days of delivery. We're unable to offer online exchanges.
We aim to process refunds as soon as possible, however the complete process can take up to 14 days.

You will find a prepaid return label in your package. If you are unable to locate this label, then you can generate one here below. Simply log in to your account or as a guest and follow the steps to print at home.

To reimburse you fully, these conditions must be met:

  • The gear is returned in its original condition and in its original packaging
  • The original labels are attached to the clothing
  • Accessories are returned with all parts included in the original packaging
  • Shoes are returned in the original shoe box

Please note that electronic products, underwear and face masks can only be returned if found to be defective. If the protective liner of underwear has been removed, the product can't be returned or replaced for health protection reasons. Please note that full shipping costs, will not be included in the refund, unless the gear was faulty or defective when it arrived.



We can't reimburse if your gear has been damaged due to negligence, misuse or wear and tear. We can't refund if the gear wasn't purchased in our Online Shop or in one of our official stores.
